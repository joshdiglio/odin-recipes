Peanut Butter & Jelly

How to make a classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Did you know that there’s a certain way to make a pb&j even better? It’s true. Keep reading!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite type of peanut butter
  • Your favorite type of jelly
  • Your favorite type of bread

Steps

  1. Apply peanut butter to both sides of the bread
  2. Apply a layer of jelly on top of both peanut buttered sides
  3. Put both pieces of bread together to form a sandwich
