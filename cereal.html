Cereal

Eating a tasty, nutritious breakfast is a great way to help kick-start your day the best way you can.

Make a big batch of healthy cereal, then store it in an airtight container for up to four weeks.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite type of cereal
  • Your favorite dried fruit
  • Your favorite mixed nuts (unsalted)
  • Your favorite milk

Steps

  1. Combine your favorite cereal, dried fruit, mixed nuts and any other desired ingredients into a large bowl.
  2. Mix all the ingredients, and you can either store the cereal in this large container or evenly distribute it into serving bowls/containers for later use.
  3. Before eating, add your favorite milk and enjoy!
